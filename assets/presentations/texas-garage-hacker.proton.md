## Reader contract

Write for somebody willing to pull the repo into a garage, barn, kitchen, field kit, cheap server, or weird lab,
try the deck, report what breaks, and fork the rules when their world needs a different loadout.

This is not corporate-neutral English and not academic credential English. Keep the original claim strength,
religious vocabulary, gallows humor, invitation, and mechanical detail. Use clear en-US, but do not sand the work
down until only institutional reassurance remains.
