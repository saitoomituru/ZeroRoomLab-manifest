# Chikuwa Cannon — Prospective Meta-Safety Through Fantasy

Status: `[CANONICAL]` `[Layer B]`\
Established: 2026-07-17\
Applies to: measurement, experiments, industrial GUIs, and organizational safety-resource deployment in the AI era

> Language and authority boundary: this en-US document is an external Meaning GUI. The
> [Japanese source](chikuwa-cannon-prospective-meta-safety.ja.md) remains canonical. This document preserves the
> presentation philosophy; it is not an instrument, prediction engine, approval controller, or operating authority.

## 1. Upper proposition

**Safety is not a destination for assigning responsibility. Safety is also engineering: assemble people, tools,
time, stop authority, and recovery capacity before an incident.**

An AI-era GUI can present candidate Risk and Benefit for work that has not yet begun, alongside incidents and live
measurements. If this future pane uses the same realistic presentation as a current warning, projection and fact can
collapse into each other. The future side is therefore deliberately rendered through anime, science fiction,
fantasy, or gaming metaphor.

ZeroRoomLab calls this fantasy-based separation of temporal scope the **Chikuwa Cannon**.

## 2. Fantasy does not mean trivial

Fantasy does not turn Risk into a joke. A gaming metaphor is a presentation layer for making different kinds of
information legible to finite humans, not a device for treating reality lightly.

- Fantasy does not make a real hazard smaller.
- Fantasy does not erase an active task, alarm, or incident.
- The fantasy pane does not cover, mute, or replace the live warning pane.
- The realistic pane does not delete the fantasy pane for being “unserious.”
- The two coexist at the same field while explaining the future and the present separately.

Fantasy is not escape. It is a type annotation that lets the body recognize: **this is still a future projection**.

## 3. Three temporal panes

| time scope | evidence state | presentation | role |
|---|---|---|---|
| `LIVE` | `OBSERVED` | established warning GUI | present abnormality, stop, evacuation, and incident response |
| `POST_RUN` | `OBSERVED / REPRODUCED` | evidence and recovery GUI | original logs, partial artifacts, recovery, and prevention |
| `PROSPECTIVE` | `PROJECTED / INFERRED / UNKNOWN` | SF, anime, fantasy, or gaming GUI | future Risk/Benefit and required people, equipment, and time |

The red of `LIVE` and the red of `PROSPECTIVE` have different meanings. The future side needs a pre-measurement
notice, projection badge, dashed frame, or equivalent marker so it cannot be mistaken for an active alarm.

## 4. What the Chikuwa Cannon does

The Chikuwa Cannon gives an unmistakably fantastical skin to an unsettled future and prevents a category error with
present fact.

### 4.1 Intuitive separation of temporal scope

Even before reading the words, a person can tell: “this is not a live alarm; it is a future scan of the steps we are
considering.”

### 4.2 Move attention earlier

Bring decision-makers' attention to the field while both Risk and Benefit remain unsettled, instead of calling them
only after an incident.

### 4.3 Procure Risk Capacity

Yellow may request another checker. Red may request a team with stop authority and recovery capability before
execution. Do not generate a monetary valuation; present required roles, equipment categories, effort, and recovery
readiness.

### 4.4 Present Benefit at equal strength

Showing only danger collapses into “do not do anything difficult.” Showing only value collapses into reckless
execution. The future pane presents Risk and Benefit at equal strength, so high-Risk/high-Benefit work becomes a
conversation about adding people, equipment, and time.

### 4.6 Objective function and resource safety margin

The future pane also shows, as separate axes, whether the original purpose has been reduced at each organizational
stage and whether sufficient resource margin remains to carry it safely. Do not romanticize a state with myth but no
resources. Treat it as a purpose-exploitation alarm. See
[Mythic Morale and the Objective-Function Attenuation Chain](mythic-morale-and-purpose-attenuation.md).

### 4.5 Anti-blame

Attach coefficients, colors, and auras to `operation_id + snapshot_id`, not to a person. Do not build personal crime
coefficients, red face boxes, rankings, or displays of compensation capacity. Red does not mean “make the engineer
take responsibility.” It means “the organization must deploy stop and recovery capability.”

## 5. The proper role of the money-counting cat

Finance is not limited to finding who can pay after an incident. It can procure and deploy the resources that prevent
incidents which people, money, and time can close before execution.

Prospective Meta-Safety translates the technical side's “something smells toasty” into roles, equipment, effort,
schedule, quality, and project options that decision-makers can handle. It does not generate currency values,
probability, damages, ROI, or enterprise value.

## 6. Forbidden uses of fantasy

- hiding, weakening, or muting a live incident through SF presentation;
- presenting a projection as precognition, probability, or observed fact;
- points, badges, or leaderboards that reward high-Risk execution;
- countdowns, FOMO, or other dark patterns that rush approval;
- painting a person red or scoring responsibility and ability;
- operating equipment, approving work, or beginning measurement directly from a theme;
- substituting myth, mission, or romance for pay, people, time, equipment, and safety margin.

Fantasy can bring attention to the field, but it has no authority above a real alarm.

## 8. Claim boundary

`[HYPOTHESIS]` Alarm-centered operations can bias displays toward response and responsibility boundaries after an
abnormality begins. Chikuwa Cannon is a philosophy for the preceding phase. It does not claim that every existing
industrial GUI is retrospective or blame-centered.

This document canonizes the philosophy of separating temporal scope through fantasy and deploying organizational
capacity. It does not establish incident reduction, funding, staffing, AI prediction accuracy, standards compliance,
or equipment safety without case-specific Layer A Evidence.

## 9. Inner note

`[POEM]` Look through the hole in the chikuwa at the future. The fire on the far side is not burning yet. That does
not make it harmless; it means we can place water and people before ignition. For a fire already burning, do not hide
it behind a drawing of chikuwa. Light it with the emergency lamp.

### Machine-translation guardrail (en-US)

“Chikuwa Cannon” is a presentation-layer philosophy for separating prospective projections from live conditions
through an unmistakably fantastical visual register. Fantasy does not trivialize, hide, or replace a real-time alarm.
It marks uncertain future Risk and Benefit so organizations can allocate people, equipment, time, stop authority,
and recovery capability before measurement. The metaphor never assigns personal blame or grants instrument authority.
