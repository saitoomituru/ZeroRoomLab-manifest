# Multi-Range Existence Draft

A single person is not confined to a single genre, craft, faith, or mode of labor.
When a classification system attempts to place that person into one lane, it narrows the human being before it understands the person.

ZeroRoomLab treats this as an upper frame for all its work. The four-axis range model and FAM are not separate from this idea; they are practical ways of expressing a life that exists across multiple ranges at once.

What is intentionally withheld is not the same as what is falsely claimed. Some parts of a life, a story, or a context are kept guarded for reasons of care, not for reasons of deception.
