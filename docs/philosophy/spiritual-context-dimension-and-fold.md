## 1. What this document preserves

What Sphere has called the “spiritual dimension,” “dimension of spiritual kotodama,” or “higher World layer” is not
an extension above Linux, POSIX, OSI, application architecture, or another technical Layer.

A spiritual dimension is a **Context Dimension**: it retains what is being discussed, under which worldview,
ontology, observer, and ruler. Putting spiritual meaning on the same vertical axis as technical layers reduces it into
implementation hierarchy and also breaks the responsibilities of established technical terms.

```text
Technical Layer L
  counts what runs on what, and the direction of dependency

Context Dimension D
  counts how many functionally equal meaning axes are bundled sideways
```

“Functionally equal” does not mean “the same meaning.” It means that no axis is demoted into another axis's
subcategory or decoration. Each may retain its own Registry, Schema, World, fact scope, observations, and FAM.

## 2. D Fold

`D` is not the name of a meaning dimension. It is the number of unique Context Dimensions bundled into one Fold.

```text
D = |unique(context dimensions)|
```

Examples:

```text
Astro 4D Fold
├─ Cloud Chakra
├─ Spiritual Chakra
├─ Elemental
└─ Astral

Actor 4D Fold
├─ User
├─ Assistant
├─ System
└─ Vendor

World 3D Fold
├─ World
├─ Object
└─ Action

Operation 6D Fold
├─ Financial
├─ TEC
├─ Supply
├─ Vision
├─ Legal-JP
└─ Legal-US
```

Astro 4D Fold and Actor 4D Fold both contain four axes, but they are not necessarily compatible. `4D` tells you the
axis count, not identity, Registry, or mapping availability.

Context Dimensions are parallel by default; list order alone creates no superior/subordinate relation. Technical
Layers carry dependency order, so reordering them creates a different structure. Preserve that difference.

## 3. Fold crossing and manifestation

A gate, ritual, translation, summoning, manifestation, or embodiment that connects different Folds maps inside Sphere

```text

  -> Access Map / Mapping FAM
```

This mapping does not replace spiritual vocabulary with technical terms or erase the source meaning. Spiritual

## 5. One Effect, multiple interpretations

Suppose a person hits a toe on a dresser, the computer falls over, and the World disappears. Do not freeze the
observed sequence into one absolute cause.

```text
Mythology Fold
  dresser spirit, goddess of probability, trick of fate

Safety Engineering Fold
  clutter, near miss, missing fall protection, missing redundancy

World Fold
  World Config that deletes a World when its Host disappears

Physical Fold
  contact, vibration, gravity, power/runtime stop
```

Each interpretation can itself be an OAE in which an Observer or Interpreter acts on the Source Event and generates
a new causal hypothesis. Do not overwrite the original observation. Keep separate Registry, fact scope, and hypothesis.

## 6. Do not import a ruler

Sphere Architect is not the ruler that decides which theology, physics, law, World Config, or accident-analysis method
is ultimately correct.

> Sphere Architect is not an OS that hands out rulers. It records, preserves, and connects who defined a ruler,
> what they measured with it, and which Effect and hypothesis resulted.

A third-party SDK may define Context Dimensions for theology, game magic, natural science, safety engineering,
jurisdiction, business practice, identity, or World. Inside the selected Schema, it must remain faithful to Agency
roles, Effect scope, Evidence, unknowns, and transformation rules.
