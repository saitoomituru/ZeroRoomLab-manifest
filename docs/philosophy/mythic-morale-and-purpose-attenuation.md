# Mythic Morale and the Objective-Function Attenuation Chain

Status: `[CANONICAL]` `[Layer B]`\
Established: 2026-07-17\
Applies to: R&D, experiments, measurement, resource deployment, and organizational safety

> Language and authority boundary: this en-US document is an external Meaning GUI. The
> [Japanese source](mythic-morale-and-purpose-attenuation.ja.md) remains canonical. This document preserves the
> philosophy and operating questions; it does not create an executable safety controller or prove an accident model.

## 1. Upper proposition

Safety has two necessary halves.

1. **Romance, myth, dream, and philosophy** — the meaning half that keeps “why we do this,” “what we hope to gain,”
   and “what we protect” from being reduced.
2. **Resources with sufficient safety margin** — the material half that carries that romance into reality through
   people, time, equipment, budget, stop authority, and recovery capacity.

“Half” is not a measured accident percentage or contribution rate. It is a Layer B model of completeness: safety does
not close without both hemispheres. Satisfying one does not authorize omitting the other.

## 2. Myth is purpose-preservation infrastructure, not decoration

Narrative, myth, dream, romance, and philosophy do not fit completely inside KPI. They are not decoration that makes
work look exciting, and they are not fuel for extracting unpaid labor. They are meaning infrastructure that lets
people read, retell, and recover the original objective when conditions become difficult.

When the objective collapses into proxy KPI such as measurement count, deadline, or budget burn, the field loses the
reason for doing careful work. The last visible behavior may look like avoidance, corner-cutting, or failure to check.
Investigation must also ask whether purpose, expectation, and optionality were removed upstream over time.

This does not excuse every execution failure. It sets an investigation boundary: the last visible action is not
automatically the root cause.

## 3. Progressive objective-function attenuation chain

`[HYPOTHESIS]` The following chain may become an organizational failure mode that consumes safety margin and morale
at the same time.

```text
original dream / philosophy / expectation
  -> reduced to “cost” at the funding stage
  -> cut at planning without retaining purpose
  -> impossible conditions normalized by management
  -> execution loses both meaning and margin
  -> avoidance, skipped checks, or corner-cutting becomes visible
  -> incident or quality degradation
```

Funding sees execution laziness. Execution sees management pressure. Management sees an impossible plan. Planning sees
hostile demands from funding. If each stage blames only its neighbor, negative emotion and resource deficit circulate
like an ecosystem.

Do not turn this chain into a personality score. Ask where meaning was removed and which safety margin and purpose
failed to travel downstream.

## 4. Observe the two halves independently

| Meaning half | Resource half | State |
|---|---|---|
| retained | safety margin present | candidate for mapping romance safely into reality |
| retained | insufficient | **purpose-exploitation alarm**; myth must not substitute for labor or risk capacity |
| missing | safety margin present | **objective-function drift**; equipment exists, but the protected purpose became proxy KPI |
| missing | insufficient | **organizational collapse candidate**; do not fill it with grit—return purpose and resources upstream |

Do not infer an `UNKNOWN` into one of the four quadrants. Keep it as a separate confirmation request.

## 5. Boundary against purpose exploitation

Mythic morale does not substitute for:

- compensation, contracts, rest, and appropriate working hours;
- enough people, specialist roles, and cross-checks;
- probes, isolation, protective equipment, calibration, and recovery equipment;
- planning, practice, stop decisions, and room to withdraw;
- technical gates, regulatory requirements, and physical safety factors.

“If you believe in the dream you can endure,” “mission makes low staffing acceptable,” and “accept danger for the
romance” are the opposite of this philosophy.

The larger the romance, the larger the resource margin needed to land it safely in reality. Myth is not a license to
cut the margin. It is language for telling the organization why the margin is required.

## 6. Morale management is not emotional control

Morale does not mean demanding cheerful faces or positive speech. It means the people doing the work do not lose:

- the upper objective connected to the task;
- the knowledge, quality, options, and future it may create;
- the legitimacy of refusing or withdrawing;
- the right to request required resources;
- a path for the field to propose changes to the objective.

Do not erase dissatisfaction, fear, fatigue, or dissent as low morale. Treat them as signals that purpose or resources
may be missing.

The future pane in [Chikuwa Cannon](chikuwa-cannon-prospective-meta-safety.md) /
[Japanese source](chikuwa-cannon-prospective-meta-safety.ja.md) displays not only Risk, but also Benefit, original

Fantasy is not a device for inspiring people to push through impossible conditions. It is a presentation layer that
keeps the value of a future that does not yet exist intact, then gathers the material conditions required to reach it
before an incident.

## 8. Mapping into Layer A

Implementation records the purpose append-only instead of freezing it into a slogan. At each funding, planning,
management, and execution stage, record what was retained, changed, and removed.

Minimum recommended structure:

- original purpose, myth, and expected Benefit;
- non-negotiable safety controls;
- resource assumptions and safety margins;
- each stage's proposed change, reason, impact, and unknowns;
- purpose-integrity and resource-integrity signals;
- downstream acknowledgment and rollback path.

Attach signals to an operation and snapshot. Do not score human loyalty, personality, or enthusiasm.

See the Layer A [Meta-KPI Drift Verification Procedure](../theory/meta-kpi-drift.md) /
[Japanese source](../theory/meta-kpi-drift.ja.md).

## 9. Claim boundary

`[HYPOTHESIS]` A chain of purpose attenuation and resource deficit may become skipped checks or degraded quality at
the final execution stage. This document does not claim that it causes every incident or a measured fraction of them.
Accident cause, morale, resource sufficiency, and organizational culture require case-specific Layer A Evidence.

`[POEM]` Half of an accident is a shortage of romance and myth. The other half is a shortage of resources that give
that romance and myth enough safety margin. This is not a statistical pie chart. When a human builds a bridge toward
the future, neither meaning alone nor steel alone reaches the other bank.

### Machine-translation guardrail (en-US)

Myth, narrative, dreams, romance, and philosophy preserve the purpose function of difficult work; they are not
decorative “engagement” language. Material resources with sufficient safety margin are an independent, equally
necessary half. Myth must never substitute for compensation, staffing, time, equipment, recovery capacity, or
engineering controls. The “two halves” are a philosophical completeness model, not a measured accident statistic.
