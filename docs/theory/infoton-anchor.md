# Infoton Anchor

## ⚠️ Old Name "Quantum Anchor" Is Retired

The old name **"Quantum Anchor"** is gone.

"Quantum" got narrowed down to electron-spin / superconducting qubit technology by research funding politics. Using it for structural concepts (non-determinism, observation constraints, correlation structure) causes misreads.
→ Full background: [infoton-engineering.md §6](infoton-engineering.md)

---

## Definition

An Infoton Anchor temporarily fixes an observation point inside the ongoing fluctuation of observation, interpretation, subjectivity, and cultural difference.

Zero relation to physical quantum computing. It's the **foundational operation for observation point management** in Infoton Engineering.

---

## Role

- Don't throw away subjective observation as pure noise
- But don't lock it in as absolute truth either
- Hold the observer, context, time, constraints, and purpose as a set
- Forms the base of observation point management in AI, divination, edge AI, and FAM

---

## Relationship to FAM

If FAM is the map connecting different Folds, the Infoton Anchor is the stake you hammer in to say "I'm looking from THIS Fold right now."

```
Infoton       = fluctuating meaning-state (undetermined meaning)
Infoton Anchor = observation point fixed (declaring who, from where, toward what)
```

FAM needs to know which Fold you're accessing from. The Infoton Anchor is what temporarily establishes that "from where."

---

## Relationship to Infoton Engineering

Infoton Engineering deals with "undetermined meaning states" (infotons). Infotons inherently fluctuate — meaning shifts with the observer, context, and purpose.

The Infoton Anchor temporarily fixes:

- **Who** (observer / Q)
- **From where** (context / Fold)
- **Toward what purpose** (∇φ: gradient / direction)
- **Under which constraints** (λ: output layer / boundary conditions)

Declaring those makes `Q(ψ, ∇φ, λ)` computable. Without the anchor, the computation is undefined.

## Why "Infoton" Not "Quantum"

From the note article "The word 'quantum' was stolen" (2026-06-01):

> The essence of quantum theory isn't in the physical carrier "electron spin" — it's in the mathematical and information-structural properties: non-determinism, observation constraints, correlation preservation, nonlinear convergence. But "quantum" got hijacked. Research funding politics locked it to electron spin and superconducting qubits.

> What happened to the conceptual space that was left after "quantum" got narrowed? That's what "Infoton" reclaims.

The Infoton Anchor's "fixing of observation points" is an Infoton Engineering layer operation. This is not about physical quantum mechanics — it's a response to the observer problem in meaning-space.

## Three-Layer Model — Where It Sits

| Layer | Name | Observer position |
|---|---|---|
| Physical quantum | Electron spin, superconducting qubits | Outside the system |
| Quantum-inspired | Classical HW approximating quantum algorithms | Outside the system |
| **Infoton** | **Human included inside the system** | **Inside the system** |

The Infoton Anchor operates at the third layer. **The observer being inside the system** is the fundamental difference from physical quantum and quantum-inspired approaches.
